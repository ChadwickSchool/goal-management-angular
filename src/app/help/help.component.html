FAQ

How to Login?

To login go to the home page and press the "Log in with Google" button

 How can I see my Goals?

Locate to the home page. On the home page you can either press "View Goals" in order to view all your goals or you can click the "View Classes" button to select a specific class.

 How do I see my classes?

Your classes are automatically assigned to you. If you are either assigned to the wrong class or missing classes please contact us. In order to view your classes, click on the "View Classes" button on the home page. Then click a specific class on the next page.
